The Proposal

On September 7, 2024, Sahil proposed to Arielle in the stunning Yosemite National Park, surrounded by towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls — a truly perfect moment. Just a year earlier, they had summited Half Dome together, which now served as a beautiful backdrop to their special day. They are excited to share their engagement celebration with all of you!
